## App Title

## Short Description (80 chars max)
A fast, offline-first note-taking app with search and cloud sync.

## Full Description
SimpleNotes is a clean, minimal note-taking app built for people who value simplicity and reliability.

**Offline First** — Create and edit notes without an internet connection. Your notes are stored locally and sync automatically when you're back online.

**Instant Search** — Find any note in milliseconds with full-text search. No more scrolling through endless lists.

**Cloud Sync** — Sign in to sync your notes across all your devices. Powered by Supabase for fast, secure synchronization.

**Clean Design** — A warm, distraction-free interface that gets out of your way. Just you and your thoughts.

**Key Features:**
- Create, edit, and delete notes
- Full-text search across all notes
- Offline support with automatic sync
- Auto-save while typing
- Secure cloud backup via Supabase
- Dark mode support
- Material Design 3

## Privacy Policy
SimpleNotes stores notes locally on your device and optionally syncs to Supabase cloud servers when you sign in. We do not collect analytics, sell data, or show ads. Your notes are protected by row-level security — only you can access your data.
